Gregor Bachmann

I am a PhD student supervised by Prof. Thomas Hofmann at the Data Analytics Lab (Dalab) in the Department of Computer Science (D-INFK) at ETH Zurich. I am working on understanding the generalization capacities of neural networks from a mathematical perspective. Prior to joining the Data Analytics group, I obtained my Bachelor's and Master's degree in Mathematics at ETH Zurich.
